ABOUT US

Sangeetika Kalanilayam is a community where music seeks a connection with in and with each other. We provide Classical Carnatic Music, Light Music , Bhagavadgeeta Shlokas, Nitya Pooja Shlokas, Bhaagavata Padyalu, Dance, Painting & Crafts in a traditional way.


   Sangeetika is a dedicated and professional institute always ready to teach values in a musical way. Sangeetika has started its activities from 2015 and has officially registered with Govt. of Telangana in 2019.
